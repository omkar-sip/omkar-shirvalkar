# BCX – Bharat Carbon Exchange

**BCX** is a carbon credit trading platform built for India, designed to enable seamless participation in the green economy. It connects farmers, industries, and government bodies to trade verified carbon credits transparently on a digital marketplace.

## Key Features

- 🌿 **Carbon Credit Marketplace** – Buy, sell, and trade verified carbon credits
- 👨‍🌾 **Farmer Onboarding** – Enable farmers to register and earn credits for sustainable practices
- 🏢 **Industry Dashboard** – Companies can track and offset their carbon footprint
- 🔐 **Secure Authentication** – Phone OTP, Google Sign-In, and email/password support
- 📊 **Real-time Analytics** – Live carbon credit data and transaction history
- ☁️ **Firebase Backend** – Powered by Firestore and Firebase Auth

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React + TypeScript + Vite
- **State Management:** Zustand
- **Backend:** Firebase (Firestore, Auth)
- **AI Features:** Google Gemini API
